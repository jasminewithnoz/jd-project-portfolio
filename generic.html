    3D Printed Stratocaster & Precision Bass

    Final Project for CEHC Makerspace Internship during Fall 2024 Semester.

    One of my proudest projects! These fully playable instruments took around 4 months to design, print, and assemble. Orginally meant to be a Spring 2023 project, but became a Fall 2023 project! Used several different softwares within the design and build process as well as learning some new skills to assemble them. One of my crowning acheievements!

    Skills Include: Computer-Aided Design, 3D Printing, Guitar Playing, Project Management


    3D LEGO Damiens

    Made in and for CEHC Makerspace.

    These are little trinkets that are a mixture of the University at Albany's mascot, Damien and the iconic LEGO minifigure. Give multiple hats and printed in several colors, these were handed out to vistiors and guests as a way to display what can be done within our Makerspace.
    Took a couple of months to design and multiple prototypes were made.

    Skills Include: Computer-Aided Design, 3D Printing, Project Management



    The Wizard's Handbook - TCG Rules Application

    Final Project for CINF302 during Spring 2025 Semester.

    A app made to help new players learn the world of different tabletop card games (TCGs). Made and designed for both new players and long time veterans of different games to learn older and new mechanics.

    Skills Include: Wireframing and Design within Figma, User Research, Project Management


    RoamReady - Travel Itinerary Website

    Final Project for CINF362 during Spring 2025 Semester.

    A dynamic travel itinerary website made to ease the stress of traveling. Made within a group of two where I made and coded all of the frontend code including all the HTML, CSS and JavaScript while my group member handled the backend code, which included the PHP, JSON and SQL databases.

    Skills Include: Programming / Coding within HTML, CSS and JavaScript, Wireframing and Design within Figma, Project Management
